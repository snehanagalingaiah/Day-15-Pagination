Pagination DOM


The page shows details of every object in the array. There will always be 5 numbered buttons on the page. Besides this there will be first and prev buttons if it is not the first record that you are displaying. Also, next and prev buttons will be displayed if its not the last record being displayed. The button that is currently being displayed will always be in center of the button list if its not 1st,2nd or the last two records being diaplayed.
